Sales are not retained revenue

The marketplace dashboard shows sales. The bank account shows the truth.

Many brands see revenue, orders, dashboards, and ROAS. Fewer can clearly see what the business actually keeps after pricing, fees, ad spend, returns, logistics, settlements, and operational leakage.

Revenue is not growth if the system bleeds margin.

ROAS looks healthy, but margin is weak.

Ad performance is reviewed against contribution margin, conversion, SKU economics, and retained revenue.

Products sell, but SKU economics are hidden.

MASAR classifies SKUs by profitability, scalability, risk, and next commercial action.

Returns and logistics leak margin.

Leakage signals are traced across refunds, shipping, fees, commissions, and settlement gaps.
